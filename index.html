About NutriPlanr

We believe that good nutrition should be simple, personalized, and sustainable. NutriPlanr makes this vision a reality.

Our Mission

NutriPlanr was created to solve the complexity of meal planning and nutrition tracking. We understand that everyone's nutritional needs are different, and that's why our app adapts to you - not the other way around.
